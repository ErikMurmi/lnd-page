    Our History

    At EasyBank, our history is a reflection of our commitment to our customers and our passion for financial innovation. From our humble beginnings to becoming leaders in the digital banking industry, we've come a long way.

    • 2005

      EasyBank was founded with the vision of making banking more accessible and convenient for everyone.

    • 2010

      We achieved the milestone of serving one million satisfied customers.

    • 2025

      We expanded our services internationally, opening branches in 10 countries.

    • 2020

      Today, we are a trusted financial institution serving over 5 million customers worldwide.

    Our Mission and Values

    At EasyBank, our mission is to simplify finances for individuals and businesses by providing innovative and accessible banking solutions. We work tirelessly to deliver high-quality financial services and make our customers' lives easier.

    Our core values are integrity, innovation, customer commitment, and sustainability. These values guide every aspect of our work and business decisions.
